Version 1.0.0

LATEST March 2026
  • Initial Launch: The Cybat Intelligence Engine is officially available on the Google Cloud Marketplace.
  • Zero-Retention Ingestion: Pub/Sub driven architecture enables latency-free telemetry correlation with no logs hitting persistent disks.
  • Gemini Threat Models: Integrated advanced LLM heuristics tuned for specific GCP attack paths including IAM escalation and unauthorized VPC peaking.
  • Google SSO Interoperability: Organization and user-level identity management completely abstracted to Google Workspace APIs.
