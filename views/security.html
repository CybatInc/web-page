Cybat Security & Data Privacy Overview

Cybat is an AI-native SOC platform built with an "Enterprise-First" security posture. We leverage Google Cloud’s world-class infrastructure to ensure the highest levels of data protection and operational integrity.

Data Residency & Architecture

Zero-Retention: Unlike traditional SIEMs, Cybat AI evaluates logs in-memory and immediately discards them. Because there is no persistent storage of your sensitive operational data on our systems, the risk surface is fundamentally minimized.

Data Residency: All customer telemetry and metadata are stored and processed strictly within designated Google Cloud regions, ensuring compliance with data sovereignty requirements.

Encryption Standards

Cybat enforces industry-standard encryption for all data:

  • In Transit: All data moved between the Customer Project and the Cybat Partner Project is protected via TLS 1.2+.
  • At Rest: Data stored in Firestore and BigQuery is encrypted using AES-256.

Identity & Access Management (IAM)

We follow the Principle of Least Privilege. Cybat utilizes GCP Workload Identity to interact with customer environments, using granular permissions that allow only the specific actions required for automated remediation.

AI Governance

Our SOC Agent utilizes Vertex AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite). Under our enterprise agreement with Google Cloud, your data is never used to train foundation models, ensuring your proprietary logs remain private.
