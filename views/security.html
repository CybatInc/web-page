Built from day one with a zero-trust mindset. Our posture reflects the most robust security best practices expected of enterprise Google Cloud Marketplace partners.

Zero-Retention Architecture

Unlike traditional SIEMs, Cybat AI evaluates logs in-memory and immediately discards them. We intercept your Google Cloud logs via a dedicated Pub/Sub topic and deploy our autonomous agent to assess threats in milliseconds.

Because there is no persistent storage of your sensitive operational data on our systems, the risk surface is fundamentally minimized. If our infrastructure were ever compromised, there would be no historic logs to exfiltrate.

Encryption & Compliance

All communication between your GCP environment and the Cybat AI endpoint is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS 1.3 protocols.

Any configuration data and mitigation rules are secured at rest using AES-256 encryption. We abide by strict IAM policies and the principle of least privilege, ensuring our internal access to production environments is strictly gated.
