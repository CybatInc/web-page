How it Works

Cybat AI integrates natively with your Google Cloud Organization. By leveraging a high-speed, stateless architecture, we process security telemetry at scale without ever storing your sensitive logs.

  • 01

    Stage 1: Ingestion & Telemetry

    Continuous monitoring of your GCP organization. Our agents ingest high-volume telemetry from cloud storage, network logs, and compute signals through secure Pub/Sub channels.

  • 02

    Stage 2: Gemini AI Core

    Every pattern is processed by our secure Gemini 2.5 Flash inference engine. It identifies intent, classifies zero-day threats, and distinguishes bots from users in sub-seconds.

  • 03

    Stage 3: Autonomous Mitigation

    The platform executes immediate shielding. Protective rules are injected into Cloud Armor policies and VPC Firewalls, creating a global perimeter that adapts to live threats.
